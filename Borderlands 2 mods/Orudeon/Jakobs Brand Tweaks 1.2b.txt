#<Jakobs Brand Tweaks 1.2a>

    Improves competitiveness of several Jakobs guns. Gives assault rifles full-auto. Hammerlock guns now sport perfect accuracy, high crit bonuses, and DPS similar to a full Jakobs Muckamuck to give them a unified Sniper theme.

    Coded by Orudeon

    Changelog:

    V1.2:

    Lowered the Rex's crit rate and increased its base damage.

    Doubled the Elephant Gun's crit damage (260% additive).

    Doubled the Damned Cowboy's crit damage (200% additive and 30% multiplicative).

    Added the Triquetra (firing pattern by AngrierPat).

    V1.1

    Simplified Striker code and enabled Vertical Grip again. It does not gain bonus projectiles from the Vertical Grip, only its other benefits. Slightly reduced the Striker's accuracy.
